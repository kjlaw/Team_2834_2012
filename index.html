Home

Good morrow, lords and ladies!

More than just a group of medieval geeks, we are Team 2834 - the Bionic Barons of Bloomfield Hills, Michigan and we are pleased that you have chosen to visit our website.  Welcome to our castle!

This is our fourth year of competition and the third year that students of two Bloomfield Hills School District high schools, Andover and Lahser, comprise our membership and talent.

If you are not familiar with FIRST Robotics and would like to find out more about it, please visit our webpage About FIRST. If you would like to find out more about our team and what we do, please visit the webpages under Our Team. Feel free to roam around our castle, there is a lot to see, and our dragons are friendly.

If you are visiting from another FIRST Robotics Competition team, you may be interested in checking out our Resources area. We keep all our treasures there to freely share with folks from other kingdoms. If you require assistance with anything, please feel free to contact us.  We look forward to meeting you, on and off the field.
